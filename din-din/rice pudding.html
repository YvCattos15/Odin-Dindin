Rice Pudding

Rice pudding croons a lullaby of creamy comfort. It's a warm embrace of milky softness, studded with plump sultanas like sugared jewels. Spiced whispers of vanilla dance on the tongue, while a gentle ricey hum lingers for a sweet, nostalgic sigh. Whether nestled in a bowl or spooned free, rice pudding promises a cozy haven, spoonful by comforting spoonful.
