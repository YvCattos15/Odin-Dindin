Wheat Bread

A canvas of warm earth tones, wheat bread offers nutty whispers in its crust and a satisfying chew in its crumb. Denser than white, it dances between earthy sweetness and a hint of sunshine, a versatile canvas for savory fillings or the golden stage for buttery toast. From morning's crunch to a sandwich symphony, wheat bread is a symphony of flavor, texture, and wholesome goodness.
